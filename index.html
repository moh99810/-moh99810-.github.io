Mariclare's Travel Reccomendations

Costa Rica

In Costa Rica, I recommend visiting El Volcán Arenal. I stayed at a resort next to the volcano when I visited. There are no cars allowed in the resort near the volcano, so everyone gets around by using golf carts. There is a waterfall near the volcano that I walked down and the view from the bottom was amazing.

Canada

I visited Mont-Treblant, Canada last winter when the average temperature was -14 degrees. Even though it was freezing, the skiing was beautiful and the people that lived there were so welcoming. If you can bear the cold, it’s such a good place to visit in the winter. Canada also has really delicious Beavertails, which are a desert.

Washington, DC

I’ve lived in Washington, DC my entire life, and it is one of my favorite places. There is so much to do, and DC has some of the best food. I am so grateful for having been raised in DC because it has taught me so much about culture and government.
